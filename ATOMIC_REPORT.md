## 6. Published Research & Thought Leadership

### How Minotaur Capital Is Using AI to Outperform the Market
- **Published:** LinkedIn, ~2025
- Deep-dive analysis of Sydney-based hedge fund Minotaur Capital and their internal AI system **"Taurient"**
- Key findings shared:
  - Minotaur makes **10k–20k model calls/day** across **20+ LLMs** from **10+ providers**
  - AI embedded across the entire workflow: idea generation, earnings summarization, portfolio monitoring, CRM, thesis building
  - **+13.7% return vs +6.7% MSCI ACWI** in first 6 months
  - Research cost: ~50% of one junior analyst salary
- His takeaways:
  - 🔹 AI works when embedded deeply into core ops, not bolted on
  - 🔹 Model variety + per-task benchmarking matters
  - 🔹 *"AI doesn't replace investors, it frees them up to think at higher levels"*
